Questions about billing

Do I pay per AI Recruiter or per hire?
You pay per AI Recruiter seat, per month. There are no per-hire or success fees. Predictable and transparent.
Can I add or remove AI Recruiters mid-month?
Yes. You can add seats at any time — they're prorated. Removing seats takes effect at the next billing cycle. No penalties.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes. Start gives you 14 days or 10 interview-ready candidates, whichever comes first.
Is there an annual discount?
Yes — pay annually and save 20% across all seats. Use the toggle above to see annual pricing.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
You can export all your candidate data at any time. After cancellation, data is retained for 90 days then permanently deleted. You own your data, always.
Need more than 25 interview-ready candidates per month?
Choose Custom for 50+ interview-ready candidates per month, tailored workflows, onboarding, and SLA-backed delivery.
