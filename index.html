Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get an AI Recruiter up and running?
Most teams are live within 24 hours. Setup involves defining your ideal candidate profile, connecting your ATS, and reviewing the outreach templates. Our team walks you through it.
What makes this different from a job board or LinkedIn Recruiter?
Job boards wait for candidates to apply. LinkedIn Recruiter requires manual outreach. Your AI Recruiter proactively sources AND reaches out — fully automated, 24/7, at scale.
Can I add more AI Recruiters for different roles?
Yes. Each AI Recruiter is configured for a specific role or department. Add as many as you need. Each seat is billed at the same flat rate.
What channels does the AI Recruiter use for outreach?
Email, LinkedIn, and optionally SMS and WhatsApp. All messages are personalized based on the candidate's profile and your company voice.
Does it integrate with our ATS?
Yes. We integrate with Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, BambooHR, and 40+ others. New candidates and their status sync automatically.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No contracts. Month-to-month billing. Cancel anytime. Annual plans get 20% off.
